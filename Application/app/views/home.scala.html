How Laker Books Works

For Buyers:
Once you have found a textbook you are interested in and you click “Buy”, you and the seller will be put into contact through email. No transactions happen through the website so it will be up to you to decide where to meet and exchange the textbook for the price listed.

For Sellers:
Once you have posted the textbook on the website, anyone searching for that book will be able to see it. When a person has decided to buy the textbook, you will be notified through email by the individual. The textbook will automatically be taken down from the website once someone has agreed to buy the book.
